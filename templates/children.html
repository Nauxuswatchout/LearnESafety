E-Safety for Children

Online Safety Rules

Personal Information: Never share your real name, address, school, or phone number online

Strong Passwords: Use different passwords for different accounts and keep them secret

Friend Requests: Only accept requests from people you know in real life

Ask for Help: If something feels wrong or scary, tell a trusted adult right away

Being Kind Online

Digital Citizenship: Treat others online with the same respect as in person

Think Before Posting: Once something is online, it can be hard to remove

Cyberbullying: Don't participate in mean messages or sharing embarrassing photos

Be Supportive: Stand up for others who are being treated unfairly online

Internet Smart Skills

Fact Checking: Not everything you read online is true - learn to verify information

Screen Time: Balance online activities with offline hobbies and spend time with family

Privacy Settings: Learn how to use privacy settings on games and apps

Online Games: Be careful when chatting with other players you don't know
